# Profile & Password

Every user can manage their own profile, change their password, set their timezone, and manage active sessions.

## Accessing the Profile Page

Click your **avatar or name** in the top-right header, then select **Profile**.

## Editing Your Profile

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| Name | Your display name |
| Email | Your login email |
| Company | Your company name (cosmetic only) |
| Timezone | Your IANA timezone (e.g. `America/New_York`) |

Click **Save Changes** to apply.

### Timezone

Setting your timezone makes all dates and "X minutes ago" timestamps display in your local time. Choose from the full IANA timezone list in the dropdown.

## Changing Your Password

Scroll to the **Change Password** section on the Profile page:

1. Enter your **current password**
2. Enter a **new password** (minimum 8 characters)
3. **Confirm** the new password
4. Click **Update Password**

You'll see a success message. Use the new password on your next login.

::: warning
If you forget your current password, ask an Admin to reset it from the Users page, or use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page.
:::

## Two-Factor Authentication

Scroll to the **Two-Factor Authentication** section. See [2FA](/features/2fa) for setup instructions.

## Active Sessions

Scroll to the **Active Sessions** section to see all your logged-in devices. See [Sessions](/features/sessions) for details.
